Zoom Accounts #

The background: Zoom has become popular because it's easy to get started. To join a call, someone sends you a link and you just tap on it. ðŸ‘‰ðŸ“± But what if you want to create your own Zoom meeting? That's where an account comes in.

Why have a Zoom account? With a Zoom account, you can: - Create your own meeting rooms - Customize your experience - More easily use Zoom on multiple devices

Having an account with Zoom can make life as a host a lot easier. Any settings you change will save across all your devices, and you will gain special privileges and abilities. On top of all that, basic accounts are free, and it is pretty straightforward to get started at: zoom.us/signup

Take your time, and once you have an account, here's how to use it in the Zoom app on iPad.

How To

Sign In to Zoom on iPad or iPhone

  1. Tap Sign In to show the sign in form
  2. Tap Email Address and enter your email into the field
  3. Tap Password and enter your password
  4. Tap Sign In
